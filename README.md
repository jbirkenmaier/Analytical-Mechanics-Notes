# Welcome to my Analytical Mechanics Notes!

This is an open-source note-taking project aimed at undergraduate physics and engineering students (2nd/3rd semester) who are taking their first steps into Lagrangian and Hamiltonian mechanics. 

### 🗺️ Project Roadmap
This note collection is currently being **built in public**. Chapters will be uploaded and refined iteratively.

- [x] **Chapter 1: The Principle of Least Action** Mathematical preparations (Taylor expansions, Functionals), defining the Action, and deriving the Euler-Lagrange Equations step-by-step.
- [ ] **Chapter 2: Yet to be named**
  Defining $L = T - V$, recovering Newtonian mechanics, understanding generalized coordinates, and applying the math to real-world systems (Pendulums etc.).
- [ ] **Chapter 3 and onwards: Yet to be named**
  Content still not entirely planned out

### 🛠️ How to Contribute (Building in Public)
Since I am writing this live, your feedback is the most valuable asset. Whether you are a struggling student or a seasoned professor, I invite you to help me make this note collection as accessible as possible.
